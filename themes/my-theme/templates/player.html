Stat Guide

Player metrics are cumulative totals and per-game rates for the selected season scope.

  • Games: Matches the player appeared in.
  • Goals / Assists / Points: Core offensive totals (`Points = Goals + Assists`).
  • PIM: Penalty minutes taken by the player.
  • Points/Game: Offensive production rate per appearance.
  • Regular vs Playoffs: Same season split by phase to show context changes.
  • Season History: Archive of season-by-season totals across leagues/phases.
